Digital Cloud Group was founded as an incubator designed to create, grow, and launch creative new technologies in Social Media, Content Delivery, and Content Creation through capital investments and strategic partnerships spanning from the Silicon Valley to Hollywood. With a portfolio of proprietary projects and joint ventures in the technology and film industries, Digital Cloud Group has been able to position itself as a bridge between the Silicon Valley and Hollywood. The company was founded by two entertainment industry veterans, Bryan Duran, Esq. and Larry Rodriguez with a background in law, finance, technology, and filmmaking. Digital Cloud Group is also at the forefront of leading the convergence of established industries by integrating or pairing with new technologies that vertically integrate and enhance global commerce capabilities.
