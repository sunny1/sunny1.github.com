The Company

Digital Cloud Group is a bridge between Hollywood and the Silicon Valley. The company was founded as an incubator with the ability to form partnerships as well as create, grow and launch creative and new technologies in Social Media, Content Delivery and Content Creation. Our initiative is to create, connect and merge new technologies with current industries while preserving existing business models or adapting the old to the new. Our ability to focus on new companies, new technologies and emerging markets has created opportunities for existing companies wishing to adapt to new world technological advances in the way business is handled.

The Principals

Digital Cloud Group was founded by two entertainment industry veterans, Bryan Duran Esq. and Larry Rodriguez with a background in law, finance, technology, and filmmaking. For over a decade, our team has provided legal, finance, and technical expertise in the acquisition and distribution of over 500 films for companies such as Metro Goldwyn Mayer (MGM), Warner Brothers, NBC Universal, First Look Studios, and Starz Media. In various capacities, our team was directly involved in day to day operations of home entertainment distributors, major Hollywood Studios, and independent film production companies. Titles that our team has been involved with include: The Matrix, The Grinch, Van Wilder 2, The Bourne Identity, Fast and The Furious, A Beautiful Mind, About A Boy, American Pie, I Could Never Be Your Woman, I Spit On Your Grave, and many more.
